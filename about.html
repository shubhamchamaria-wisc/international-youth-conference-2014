About IYC

JNS-IYC carries the history of eight years; it culminates the legends of eight journeys. The founding members of IYC take pride in not emulating yet another MUN but crafting an all-exclusive simulated diplomatic conference where resolutions precede issues and actions succeed ideas. The uniqueness of IYC also lies in the fact that is organized completely by a democratically elected student body and Organization Committee. The backstage organization of the event is youth-led, youth-determined and youth- created. Students are the leading speakers and thinkers at JNS-IYC- be it conducting the committee or coordinating it.

Its humble first chapter saw the opening of two committees- G8 and G20 and at the threshold of 2014, we have five committees to offer. The burgeoning number of registrations has only filled the committee halls with both national and international students with representatives from Germany and The Netherlands. We hope to see you amongst this international audience on 4th and 5th October, 2014.
